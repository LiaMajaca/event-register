The Problem: The 'Lost Proof'

High Activity Volume: Students attend dozens of bootcamps, hackathons, and workshops.

No Verified Record: Paper attendance lists are lost; existing proofs are just PDFs.

Missed Opportunities: Students struggle to prove 'soft skills' and extra-curricular engagement to employers.

The Solution: Digital Proof

NFT-Based Badges: Attendance is minted as a permanent digital asset.

Tamper-Proof: Smart contracts ensure badges cannot be faked.

Student Owned: Badges live in the student's wallet, creating a lifelong portfolio.
